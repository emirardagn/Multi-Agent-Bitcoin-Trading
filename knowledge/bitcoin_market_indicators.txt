Bitcoin Market Indicators:
1. Price Indicators:
   - Current Price
   - 24h High/Low
   - Market Cap
   - 24h Volume
   - Price Change (24h)

2. Technical Indicators:
   - RSI (Relative Strength Index)
   - MACD (Moving Average Convergence Divergence)
   - Bollinger Bands
   - Moving Averages (50, 100, 200)
   - Volume Profile

3. Market Sentiment Indicators:
   - Fear & Greed Index
   - Social Media Sentiment
   - Whale Movements
   - Exchange Inflows/Outflows
   - Futures Market Data

4. On-chain Metrics:
   - Active Addresses
   - Transaction Volume
   - Hash Rate
   - Mining Difficulty
   - Network Value to Transaction Ratio (NVT)
